title: 'Bring Your Own Data'
description: 'MocklyAPI gives you complete control over data'
icon: 'square-binary'
---


MocklyAPI support you own data without using out ai chat feature. This is useful when you have already prepared dataset.
You also have freedom to edit ai generated data to your liking.

To enable BYOD toggle it on the editor page and code editor will be enabled to edit.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/byod-switch.png" alt="BYOD switch." style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

<Note>When you enable BYOD option ai chat messages will be disabled. To enable it turn BYOD off.</Note>
