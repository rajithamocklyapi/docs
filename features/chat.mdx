MocklyAPI ships with ai chant assistant to generate mock data based on your needs to accelerate your journey in api building.

You can start by chatting with our ai agent.

<Note>When you enable BYOD option ai chat messages will be disabled. To enable it turn BYOD off.</Note>

</Frame>

<Note>We limit number of input characters based on your pricing plan.</Note>

<Frame style={{ marginTop:'1rem' }}>
  <img src="/images/chat-output.png" alt="Chat input." style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

Once you submit a chat message ai assistant will generate data based on the requirement and then populate generated data in the right side editor.

<Warning>AI assistant can sometimes get things wrong and might be unable to understand your request properly</Warning>

Once you are happy with the data save endpoint to persist data which then will be available through the endpoint.
